** The new and improved #1 Fantasy Basketball Extension **

Fantasy Basketball Wizard Chrome Extension is FOR Fantasy Basketball lovers, created BY Fantasy Basketball lovers. We designed the extension make your life easier and to help you win your league. This extension adds Fantasy Wizard tools directly into your webpage!
Features:

Highlights:
• Yahoo and ESPN support for 2018 - 2019 season
• Fantasy Wizard tools added directly into the ESPN and Yahoo Fantasy Basketball webpages
• *FANTASY WIZARD WEEKLY MATCHUP PROJECTIONS* on Matchups page - (ESPN & Yahoo) - (Standard 9-Cat - More league support coming soon)
• Individual player’s number of games for the week - (View on any week for the entire season)
• LIVE updates to games remaining for the week for each player
• Total games for the week on multiple pages
• Actively updated! New features and updates released often!


*NEW* Features added in version 3.0 and above:

• ESPN’s website redesign compatible for 2018-2019 season
